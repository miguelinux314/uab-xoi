The last part of this guide proposes 9 complex problems called missions
with a retrofuturistic sci-fi flavor. These problems vertebrate the contents of the previous
parts with a focus on the applications and the goal of reaching deep concept understanding.

Students are encouraged to work all these missions in order, and to produce solutions
in the shape of formal reports. They are also encouraged to shared them with their
immediate learning community (e.g., their class) and to exchange feedback
with formal letters.
